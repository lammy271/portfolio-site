about me

Anny Long raised and based in the Bay area is a designer interested in products, Identity, systems, packaging and UX design.

Collaboration energizes me; I thrive working across teams with many disciplines—this has led to various projects such as Google Tone, community exhibitions, and The Children's Creativity Museum. In my downtime, I watch bad 80s films for the set design, and I'm a lover of all things pixel and coffee.

about me

I'm Anny Long, an interdisciplinary designer interested in products, Identity, systems, packaging and UX design. I graduated from CCA in Communication Design, and I am currently based in San Francisco. Collaboration energizes me; I thrive working across teams with many disciplines—this has led to various projects such as Google Tone, community exhibitions, and The Children's Creativity Museum. In my downtime, I watch bad 80s films for the set design, and I'm a lover of all things pixel and coffee.
